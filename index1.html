About Me


Hi my name is Wendy, I couldn't be happier on where my life journey has taken me now Coding! Born in the Dominican Republic, I was lucky enough to always explore, to learn and share. In my later years I formalized my education in NYC, loving the art and design. I graduated from Parsons School of Design majoring in Fashion Design. Throughout my work experience I've worked with all major fashion labels across. Loved my experience and very much cherished every single memory, milestone, and accomplishments that I achieved.I was always curious to explore ,to challenge myself to unknown and ultimately thread together the new with the old.Coding was always in my sight. Now, with this changing world being able to intertwined design and coding really interested me furthermore presented a challenge in which I can myself achieved and succeed in. I'm thrilled and blessed for this opportunity and all the doors it can create for me and my career path.
