About us

Our Purpose it's to provide information to consumers about the Fast fashion Industries impact and effect it has on our environnment. We will provide companies of the Fashion Industry that have the best environmental program. In these environmental changing times it's vital we do our part to save the planet and it's resources . Our purpose it to continue creating momentum towards this movement by educating and taking proactive steps towardsthe cause. As consumers, what and how we buy can positivity influence the supply chain. Thoughful purchasing benefits the environment and reduces waste. Buy less, Recycle or Donate your clothes when you're over them. Buy Less, choose well, make it last. Your clothes shouldn't cost the earth.
